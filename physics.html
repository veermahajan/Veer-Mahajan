    Introduction to Physics




  • In this video, you will learn a bit about a fundamental part of physics - vectors and scalars. Vectors are things with direction and magnitude, like velocity, and scalars only have a magnitude, like speed. An easy way to remember this is to remember this: vectors (starts with "V") are like velocity (starts with "V"), and scalars (starts with "S") are like speed (starts with "S").
