Quick Quiz: Ancient Egypt

Practice what you learned about Ancient Egypt!

  1. Where does the Mediterranean Sea border Egypt? (North, South, etc.)

    Egypt is bordered by the Mediterranean Sea to the north.


  2. The Nile River is split into two parts. What are they?

    The White Nile, which begins in Uganda, and the Blue Nile, which begins in Ethiopia.


  3. Fill the blank: The Ancient Egyptians looked up to pharaohs because they were believed to be human versions of the god _____.

    Egyptians believed that pharaohs were human versions of the god Horus.


  4. List some of the goods that Ancient Egypt imported.

    Some imports included timber, incense, copper, gold, and luxury items such as lapis lazuli.


  5. List some of the goods that Ancient Egypt exported.

    Some exports included wheat, barley, linen, and papyrus.
